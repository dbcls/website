Database Center for Life Science (DBCLS)/Joint Support-Center for Data Science Research/ROIS has launched the JBI (Japan alliance for Bioscience Information) portal site in collaboration with National Bioscience Database Center (NBDC)/JST, DNA Data Bank of Japan (DDBJ)/National Institute of Genetics/ROIS, Protein Data Bank Japan (PDBj)/Institute for Protein Research/Osaka University.
This portal site provides one-stop services of databases and tools which are helpful in various scenes of the life science research. Visit the website (http://jbioinfo.jp/) for more detail.
