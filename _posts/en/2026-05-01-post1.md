A research article “ChatTogoVar: a TogoVar-based retrieval-augmented generation system for precise genomic variant interpretation" was published in Human Genome Variation. Nobutaka Mitsuhashi, Dr. Toyofumi Fujiwara and Dr. Atsuko Yamaguchi in DBCLS have contributed to this work.
The article is open access and is available via the DOI link below.

Mitsuhashi, N., Fujiwara, T. & Yamaguchi, A. ChatTogoVar: a TogoVar-based retrieval-augmented generation system for precise genomic variant interpretation. Hum Genome Var (2026).<br/>
DOI: [10.1038/s41439-026-00344-4](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41439-026-00344-4)
