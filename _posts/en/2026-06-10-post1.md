At the National Institute of Genetics, in order to provide domestic and overseas researchers with the opportunity to collaborate, NIG-JOINT offers “Joint Research” and “Research Meeting” between researchers inside and outside of NIG.

**Additional Open Call: NIG-JOINT Collaborative Research (A) for FY2026**<br/>
Application period: February 13, 2026 (Fri) – January 29, 2027 (Fri)

For more details, please visit the website of the National Institute of Genetics: [https://www.nig.ac.jp/en/researchers/nig-joint/](https://www.nig.ac.jp/en/researchers/nig-joint/)
